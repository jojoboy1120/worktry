Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about the W-9 process? Find answers below.

A W-9 form is required by the IRS so that Dundon Advisers can properly report payments made to you as part of your claim settlement. It provides your taxpayer identification number and certifies your status for tax purposes. Without a completed W-9, we are unable to process or issue your payment.

Once your W-9 has been submitted and verified, payments are typically processed within 30–60 days depending on the status of your specific claim and the court distribution schedule. You will receive a notification via email once your payment has been issued. Timelines may vary based on claim complexity and administrative review.

Your payment amount is determined by the approved claim value established during the claims administration process. You should have received a notice detailing your allowed claim amount. If you have not received this notice or have questions about your specific amount, please contact our team directly via the Contact page for personalized assistance.
